Valeria Lozovskaya

Vinyasa Flow
Meditation

Certified teacher of Ashtang Vinyasa and Prenatal Yoga (alliances of the USA and Britain)
Was blessed to born in a yogi family, from childhood and practiced hatha yoga under the guidance of her father. 6 years ago she discovered ashtanga yoga in the tradition of Pattabhi Joys, and from the first day of this amazing discovery was a devoted practical and responsible teacher of this discipline.
Valeria travels and teach between India (Mysore, Reshikesh), Bulgaria, Israel - most of the year to engage and improve the personal practice and skill of teachers, under the guidance of their gurus.
She considers as it’s her mission to distribute knowledge about ashtanga yoga as the idea of an ideal system for restoring health and improving the quality of life of residents of big cities.
She feels a special vocation in helping pregnant women. Prenatal yoga lessons are carefully and gently created to adapt the body and mind for the changes with upcoming birth.
Devoted to practice. Responsible in teaching.
